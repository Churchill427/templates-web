How It Works

The Vintage Vinyl Standard

1. Inspection

Every record undergoes a multi-point high-intensity light inspection to identify surface issues, scratches, and groove wear.

2. Pure Cleaning

Our proprietary pH-balanced ultrasonic bath reaches deep into the grooves to lift decades of micro-grit without residue.

3. Restoration

Finished with anti-static treatment and placed into archival-quality sleeves for future-proof preservation.

Questions?

Service Quick Answers

How long does ultrasonic cleaning take?

Standard individual cleaning takes 15-20 minutes. Bulk collection cleans are typically ready in 24-48 hours.

Do you offer disc flattening for warped records?

Yes! Using our professional ORB flattener, we can safely correct most warps over a controlled 4-hour cycle.

Is your appraisal recognized for insurance?

Absolutely. Our appraisals follow USPAP guidelines and are widely accepted by insurance carriers for rare collector items.
