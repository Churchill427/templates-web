Trust & Safety

Your Safety is Our Priority

Follow these simple rules for a safe and successful trading experience on Listify.

For Buyers

  • Meet the seller in a public, well-lit place.
  • Inspect the item thoroughly before paying.
  • Don't share your financial details or OTP with anyone.
  • If a deal sounds too good to be true, it probably is.

For Sellers

  • Provide accurate descriptions and high-quality photos.
  • Be honest about the condition of your item.
  • Avoid accepting payments via QR code screenshot or suspicious links.
  • Always verify the payment in your bank app before handing over the item.
