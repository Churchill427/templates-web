Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using Listify.

For Buyers

How do I contact a seller? ▾
Click "Call Seller" or "Send Message" on any listing page. Messages go through Listify's secure chat system. Never send advance payment before seeing the item in person.
Is Listify free to use? ▾
Yes! Browsing and contacting sellers is 100% free. Creating a basic account is free. We offer optional paid plans for sellers who want more visibility, but buying is always free.
Are the listings verified? ▾
Sellers with a ✓ badge have been ID-verified. We also have an AI system that flags suspicious listings. Always meet in public and inspect before paying.

For Sellers

How do I post an ad? ▾
Click "Post Ad" → fill in title, description, price and photos → publish. Your ad goes live instantly for free. Takes under 60 seconds!
How many free ads can I post? ▾
Free accounts get 3 active listings. Pro gets 50 listings. Business gets unlimited. Upgrade anytime from your dashboard.
How do I make my ad appear at the top? ▾
Upgrade to Pro or Business plan and use the Featured Listing boost. Featured ads appear at the top of search results and category pages, getting 5x more views than regular listings.

Safety & Payments

How do I report a suspicious listing? ▾
Click the "🚨 Report this ad" link on any listing page. Our safety team reviews all reports within 24 hours. You can also email safety@listify.in directly.
Does Listify handle payments? ▾
No — Listify is a classified ads platform. Payments are made directly between buyers and sellers. We recommend UPI, bank transfer or cash in hand. Never pay through unknown links or methods.

Still need help?

Our support team replies within 2 hours on business days.
