Buy & Sell Safely
on Listify

Your safety is our top priority. Follow these guidelines every time.

🛡️ For Buyers

Always Meet in Public

Meet in busy public spaces — malls, markets, police stations. Never go to isolated private locations with strangers.

Inspect Before Paying

Test electronics, verify vehicle documents, check the item thoroughly. Pay only after you're fully satisfied.

Never Pay Advance

Advance payment requests are the #1 scam signal. Always pay in person after seeing the item.

🔒 For Sellers

Verify Buyer Identity

Check the buyer's Listify profile, ratings and reviews. Ask for their full name before arranging a meeting.

Use Secure Payments

Accept UPI, bank transfer or cash in hand. Avoid cheques, unknown payment apps or any unusual methods.

Protect Your Data

Never share Aadhaar, PAN, bank details or OTPs. No legitimate buyer ever needs this information.

🚨

Spotted a Scam? Report It.

Our safety team reviews every report within 24 hours and takes immediate action against fraudulent sellers.
