FAQ

Common Questions

Can I bring my own records?

Absolutely! We encourage you to bring your own collection. We just ask that you allow us to give them a quick carbon-brush clean before they touch our reference styli.

Do you provide headphones?

Yes, we provide high-end Audeze and Meze planar magnetic headphones. You are also welcome to bring your own if you prefer.

Is booking required?

Walk-ins are welcome but subject to availability. We highly recommend booking in advance, especially for "The Suite" or weekend sessions.
