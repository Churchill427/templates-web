FAQ

Common Questions

How long does cleaning take?

Basic cleaning takes about 15 minutes. Deep ultrasonic cleaning takes 30 minutes per record. Turnaround for bulk orders is typically 2-3 business days.

Is ultrasonic cleaning safe for all records?

Yes! Our 40kHz ultrasonic process is completely safe for standard vinyl records. We do not recommend it for shellac 78s — we use a gentler process for those.

Can cleaning fix scratches?

Cleaning cannot repair physical scratches, but it can dramatically improve playback by removing grime that amplifies surface noise. Many "scratched" records sound much better after a deep clean.
