Our Mission

Democratizing Access to Unique & Rare Items

BidMaster was founded in 2020 with a simple belief: everyone deserves access to extraordinary items at fair prices. We built a transparent, secure platform where buyers and sellers connect in real-time.

Today, we're India's fastest-growing online auction platform with over 50,000 registered users and 2,500+ active auctions at any given time.
