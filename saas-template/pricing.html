FAQ

Common questions

Can I switch plans later? +

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades take effect at the end of your billing period.

Is there a free trial? +

Every paid plan comes with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. You can explore all features before committing.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) as well as PayPal, bank transfers for Enterprise, and crypto on request.

What happens to my data if I cancel? +

Your data is yours. You can export everything before canceling. We retain data for 30 days after cancellation in case you want to come back.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education? +

Yes — we offer 50% off for qualifying nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact our sales team to apply.
