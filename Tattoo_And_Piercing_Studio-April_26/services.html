Services FAQ

Do I need a deposit for a tattoo session?

Yes, we require a $50 - $100 non-refundable deposit to secure your booking. This amount is deducted from the final price of your tattoo.

How long does a typical piercing take?

The procedure itself takes about 1-2 minutes. However, we allocate 20 minutes per appointment for consultation and jewelry selection.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. We also offer financing for large-scale tattoo projects.
