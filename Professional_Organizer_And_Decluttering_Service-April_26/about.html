About SpaceCraft

Bringing calm and order to homes and offices since 2015. Discover the people and passion behind our mission.

Our Story

From Chaos to Calm — Our Journey

SpaceCraft was born from a simple belief: an organized space leads to an organized mind. Founded in 2015 by Emma Richardson, a former interior designer, the company began with a single mission — to help overwhelmed homeowners reclaim their spaces and their sanity.

What started as a one-woman operation has grown into a team of 12 certified professional organizers serving the greater Portland metro area. We've organized over 2,500 spaces and helped nearly 2,000 clients discover the transformative power of decluttering.

Our approach is rooted in empathy, not judgment. We understand that clutter is often tied to life transitions — moves, losses, growing families. We meet you where you are and guide you toward where you want to be.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower individuals and families to live more intentionally by creating organized, functional spaces that reflect their values and support their daily lives.

Our Vision

A world where every person feels at peace in their own home — where spaces inspire creativity, calm, and connection rather than stress and overwhelm.

Our Philosophy

Beyond Just Cleaning — We Create Systems

We believe that organization is a skill, not a personality trait. Our job isn't just to make your house look pretty for a photo; it's to build intuitive, sustainable systems that work for *your* specific brain and lifestyle.

  • Sustainability First: Systems that are easy to maintain even on your busiest days.
  • Judgment-Free Zone: We meet every client with empathy, patience, and complete confidentiality.
  • Holistic Approach: Considering your mental well-being alongside your physical space.
