Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a cleaning session take?
A standard home clean takes 2-3 hours depending on size. Deep cleans may take 4-5 hours. We'll give you an accurate estimate during booking.
Do I need to be home during cleaning?
No. Many clients provide a key or door code. All our cleaners are background-checked and insured for your peace of mind.
Are your products safe for pets?
Absolutely. All our products are certified pet-safe, child-safe, and hypoallergenic. No harsh chemicals ever.
