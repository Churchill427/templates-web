About SpaceCraft

Bringing calm and order to homes and offices since 2015. Discover the people and passion behind our mission.

Our Story

From Chaos to Calm — Our Journey

SpaceCraft was born from a simple belief: an organized space leads to an organized mind. Founded in 2015 by Emma Richardson, a former interior designer, the company began with a single mission — to help overwhelmed homeowners reclaim their spaces and their sanity.

What started as a one-woman operation has grown into a team of 12 certified professional organizers serving the greater Portland metro area. We've organized over 2,500 spaces and helped nearly 2,000 clients discover the transformative power of decluttering.

Our approach is rooted in empathy, not judgment. We understand that clutter is often tied to life transitions — moves, losses, growing families. We meet you where you are and guide you toward where you want to be.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission
