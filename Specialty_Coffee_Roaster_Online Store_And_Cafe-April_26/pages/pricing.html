Subscription FAQ

Can I change my plan later?
Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or switch between monthly and yearly billing at any time from your dashboard. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.
Is there a commitment or contract?
No contracts, no commitments. You can pause, skip a delivery, or cancel your subscription at any time with zero fees.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes! We ship to 30+ countries. International shipping rates vary by location and are calculated at checkout. Free shipping applies to US domestic orders.
What if I don't like the coffee I receive?
We have a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your order, contact us within 7 days and we'll send a replacement or issue a full refund — no questions asked.
