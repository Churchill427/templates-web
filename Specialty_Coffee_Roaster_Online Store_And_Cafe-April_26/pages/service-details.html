Our Flagship Service

Small-Batch Specialty Roasting

Every bean tells a story. Our roasting process is designed to honor the hard work of farmers and unlock the full potential hidden within each green coffee lot.

Using our Probat P25 drum roaster and Cropster software, we develop custom roast profiles for each origin. Our Q-grader-led team cups every batch before it ships, ensuring only coffees scoring 84+ on the SCA scale ever reach our customers.

  • Custom profiles for each origin
  • Batch sizes from 5kg to 100kg
  • Same-day packing in nitrogen-flushed bags
  • SCA 84+ quality guarantee
  • Full lot traceability and transparency

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What roast levels do you offer?
We offer light, medium, medium-dark, and dark roasts. Each origin is roasted to the profile that best highlights its unique flavor characteristics. Our roasters work closely with our Q-graders to determine the ideal roast level for each lot.
How fresh is your coffee when it arrives?
All coffee is roasted to order and shipped within 48 hours. We use nitrogen-flushed bags to lock in freshness. Most domestic orders arrive 3-5 days after roasting, ensuring peak flavor.
Do you offer sample packs?
Yes! Our Discovery Box includes 4 x 100g bags of our current featured origins for $29.99. It's the perfect way to explore different flavors before committing to a full bag or subscription.
Can I customize my subscription?
Absolutely. You can choose your roast level, grind size (whole bean or ground), quantity, and delivery frequency. You can also pause, skip, or cancel anytime with no fees.
What are your wholesale minimum order quantities?
Our wholesale program starts at 5kg per order. We offer tiered pricing with better rates at 10kg, 25kg, and 50kg+ volumes. Contact our wholesale team for a custom quote.
