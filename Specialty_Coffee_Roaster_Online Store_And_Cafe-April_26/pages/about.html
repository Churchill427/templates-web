Our mission is to source the finest single-origin beans from sustainable farms, roast them in small batches to unlock their full potential, and deliver unforgettable flavors to your cup — whether you visit our cafe or order online.

The Ember & Brew Way

Our Craft & Philosophy

The unwavering values that define every roast, every brew, and every relationship we build.

Ethical Sourcing

We go beyond fair trade. By establishing direct trade relationships, we ensure environmental stewardship and a prosperous future for coffee-growing communities.

Roasting Excellence

Precision is our hallmark. Every lot is meticulously cupped by our experts to develop bespoke profiles that celebrate the bean’s unique terroir and complexity.

A Shared Experience

Coffee is the catalyst for connection. From our cozy atmosphere to our immersive workshops, we strive to foster a community where every guest feels at home.

A Brief History

From a humble garage roastery to a beloved community coffee brand.

2015 — The Spark

Founded by two coffee-obsessed friends with a second-hand Probat roaster in a converted garage. Our first customers were neighbors who followed the aroma.

2017 — First Cafe

Opened our flagship cafe in the arts district. The 800 sq ft space quickly became the neighborhood's favorite meeting spot.

2019 — Going Direct

Established direct trade partnerships with farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, Guatemala, and Kenya. Started our online store and subscription service.

2021 — Roastery Expansion

Moved to a 5,000 sq ft dedicated roasting facility with state-of-the-art equipment, a cupping lab, and a training center.

2024 — National Recognition

Named "Best Specialty Roaster" by Coffee Review. Launched nationwide shipping and wholesale partnerships with 50+ cafes.
