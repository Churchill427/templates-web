About AquaLux

Crafting unforgettable ocean experiences since 2009.

From a Single Boat to a Fleet of Dreams

Founded in 2009 with a single 32-foot sailboat and a passion for the sea, AquaLux has grown into one of Miami's most trusted yacht charter services. What started as weekend sailing trips for friends has evolved into a full-service luxury maritime experience company.

Today, our fleet includes over 85 premium vessels — from intimate sailboats to mega-yachts — serving thousands of clients each year across the Caribbean, Mediterranean, and beyond. Our mission remains unchanged: to deliver unmatched quality, safety, and unforgettable memories on the water.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide world-class yacht charter experiences that combine luxury, safety, and environmental responsibility, making premium sailing accessible to adventurers worldwide.

Our Vision

To become the world's most beloved yacht charter company — known for exceptional vessels, impeccable service, and creating lifelong memories on the water.

Sustainability

We are committed to protecting the oceans we love. 30% of our fleet runs on hybrid engines, and we actively support marine conservation initiatives.

Our Values

Excellence in every detail. Safety without compromise. Genuine hospitality. Environmental stewardship. Innovation in maritime experiences.
