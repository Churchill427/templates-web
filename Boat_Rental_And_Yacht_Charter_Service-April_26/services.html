FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

We offer a diverse fleet including luxury motor yachts, sailing yachts, catamarans, speedboats, and fishing vessels. Our fleet ranges from 30ft to 120ft, accommodating 2 to 50 guests. Each vessel is maintained to the highest standards and regularly inspected for safety.
No experience is necessary! All our charter packages include a professional skipper and crew. If you'd like to captain your own vessel, we also offer bareboat charters for those with valid sailing certifications.
We offer flexible cancellation. Cancel up to 14 days before for a full refund, 7-14 days for a 50% refund. Cancellations due to severe weather are always fully refunded. Trip insurance is available at booking.
Our Voyager and Prestige packages include gourmet catering with a private chef. Explorer packages include basic refreshments. Custom catering can be arranged for any package at an additional cost.
Safety is our top priority. All vessels carry life jackets, first aid kits, fire extinguishers, and emergency communication equipment. Our crews are trained in maritime safety and first aid. We hold ISO 9001 certification and comply with all Coast Guard requirements.
