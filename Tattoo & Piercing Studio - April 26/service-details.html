Custom Tattoo Design

Your Vision, Our Expertise

Every custom tattoo starts with your story. Whether you have a detailed concept or just a spark of an idea, our artists work closely with you to create a completely original design that's uniquely yours. No flash, no copies — just authentic art made for you.

What to Expect

  • Free consultation — Discuss your ideas, placement, and sizing
  • Custom design creation — Your artist develops original artwork
  • Unlimited revisions — We refine until you're 100% satisfied
  • Sterile session — Premium inks, single-use needles, medical-grade equipment
  • Aftercare included — Products + free touch-up within 3 months

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the design process take?

Most custom designs take 3-7 days depending on complexity. Rush orders available with 48-hour turnaround.

Can I bring my own design?

Absolutely! Bring reference images, sketches, or ideas. Our artists will adapt and refine them to work beautifully as tattoos.

What styles are available?

We specialize in Traditional, Neo-traditional, Realism, Japanese, Geometric, Minimalist, Watercolor, and Blackwork styles.
