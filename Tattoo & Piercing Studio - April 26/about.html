Who We Are

More Than a Studio — We're a Family

Founded in 2010 by Marcus Steel, INK STUDIO began as a small passion project in a converted garage. What started with one artist and a dream has evolved into one of LA's most respected tattoo studios, with five resident artists and a reputation for excellence.

We believe every tattoo tells a story. Our mission is to ensure that story is told beautifully, safely, and with the respect it deserves. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques to deliver art that stands the test of time.

Our Mission

To create meaningful body art that empowers self-expression while maintaining the highest standards of safety, hygiene, and artistic integrity.

Our Vision

To be the world's most trusted tattoo studio, where artistry, innovation, and human connection come together to create lasting impressions.

Our Values

Safety first. Art always. We treat every client like family, every design as a masterpiece, and every session as a sacred exchange of trust.

Our Safety Standards
