Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will my ad appear featured?
Featured placement is activated instantly after payment confirmation. Your listing moves to the top of search results within seconds — no waiting period.
Can I feature multiple ads at once?
+
Yes. You can feature as many listings as you want simultaneously. Each ad is featured independently and billed separately. Pro plan members get 3 featured slots included monthly.
What happens when my featured period ends?
+
Your ad simply reverts to a standard (free) listing. It remains active and visible in search results — just without the premium placement. You can renew at any time.
Do featured listings work for all categories?
+
Yes — featured listings are available across all categories including vehicles, real estate, electronics, furniture, and services. Results are consistently strong across all categories.
Is there a refund if my item doesn't sell?
+
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all featured plans if you haven't received any inquiries. The Quick Sell Bundle comes with a full guarantee if your item doesn't sell in 48 hours.
