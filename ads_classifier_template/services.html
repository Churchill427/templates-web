Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free to post ads on Listify?
Yes! Basic listings are completely free — no hidden charges, no commission, no subscription required. You only pay if you want premium features like featured placement or the Quick Sell Package.
How do I get the Verified Seller badge?
Upload a valid government ID (Aadhaar, PAN, or Passport) through your account settings. Our team verifies it within 24–48 hours and adds the badge to your profile and all your listings.
